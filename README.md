The main objective of our Oh My Code! (OMC) project is to develop PharmaTrack, a comprehensive caretaker reminder and support system that assists individuals with mental impairments and caregivers in managing their daily responsibilities more effectively. The specific objectives of this project are: 

To develop a medication reminder system that helps caregivers and their dependents take medications on time and according to prescribed schedules. 

To provide an appointment management feature that allows users to schedule, track, and receive reminders for hospital and healthcare appointments. 

To create a social networking platform where caregivers and individuals with conditions such as Autism and ADHD can share experiences, seek support, and build a supportive community. 

To improve the daily caregiving experience by reducing missed medications, forgotten appointments, and difficulties in accessing support resources.
